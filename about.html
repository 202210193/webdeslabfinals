WHO WE ARE

The people's workshop

BENCH is the Philippines' largest homegrown Lifestyle and Fashion Apparel brand that offers premium quality products at affordable prices for everybody, every day. Throughout the years, BENCH has achieved the distinction of setting up shop in over 600 locations in the Philippines and has built a worldwide network of stores and outlets, reaching as far as the United States, the Middle East, and China. Keep up to date with the latest styles and trends when you shop and #LiveLifeWithFlavor with BENCH.
